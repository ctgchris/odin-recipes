Pho

Description: Pho is the best soup in the world. Best served on a cold winter day because it warms you up.

Ingredients

  • beef broth
  • beef
  • noodles
  • bean sprout

Steps

  1. Make broth
  2. Boil noodles
  3. Add meat to broth
  4. Add noodles and broth to bowl
  5. Add bean sprouts on top
