Fried Rice

Description: Fried Rice is fire. Great tasting and affordable and is fully customizable and filling.

Ingredients

  • meat
  • rice
  • frozen vegetables
  • cooking oil
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. add oil to pan
  2. add meat to pan and cook
  3. add cooked rice to pan next
  4. microwave frozen vegetables
  5. add frozen vegetables to pan
  6. mix it up
  7. add soy sauce
  8. serve
