Fried Rice

Description: Ramen is fire. Great tasting and affordable and is fully customizable and filling.

Ingredients

  • Cup Noodle
  • Water

Steps

  1. open cup noodle and remove packets
  2. tear open packets and add to cup noodle
  3. boil water
  4. add boiling water to cup noodle
  5. wait 3 minutes
  6. mix it up
  7. add toppings of your choice
  8. serve
